Hey, I'm Parth Batra. I'm a data scientist by profession, but a music critic by passion. I grew up in Rewari—a small town near Delhi-NCR—and I've been navigating the world of data science for about ~3.5 years now.

I kicked things off with internships at Genpact (as an AI Engineer) and Brightchamps (as a Software Engineer), and now I'm happily crunching numbers and building cool stuff as a Data Scientist at Procol (check procol.ai).

I like to think of myself as a Full Stack Data Scientist because I've done a bit of everything:

  • Deployed multiple data pipelines
  • Trained and deployed ML models
  • Created some pretty neat LLM agents
  • Wrote SQL queries that sometimes stretch up to 600-700 lines
  • Developed new microservices from scratch with CI/CD pipelines
  • Built dashboards for core business metrics for smarter, data-driven decisions

I started this blog to share my thoughts—whether it's my take on the latest tech or a quirky hot take (like why I believe R is better than Python for keeping code uniform). It might not be perfect, but it's real talk, just like a personal logger or conversation with my imaginary friend.

I graduated from BITS Pilani with a multidisciplinary degree—B.E. (Hons.) in Mechanical Engineering and M.S. (Hons.) in Mathematics. And on a fun note, one of my proudest achievements is clocking over 100k minutes on Spotify Rewind in the last three years.

When I'm not deep into data or coding, you can find me keeping up with POP-culture, the latest twitter dramas, or just finding new artists on spotify. Feel free to check out my articles.
